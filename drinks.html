Rum Cocktails!

Classic Daiquiri

Ingredients

Because there are so few ingredients in this drink, use the best you can.


  • Rum

    Most rums available at the liquor store are not overly expensive so I definitely recommend that you spend a few extra bucks for a smooth rum. Your taste buds will thank you.

  • Lime Jiuce

    Fresh is required. Period. Don’t even speak of the fake stuff. Your cocktail will taste off.

  • Simple Syrup

    Buy a bottle at the store or you can make at home. Equal parts sugar and water in a jar until dissolved. It’s so easy!


Instructions

The key to a really good daiquiri [or any cocktail for that matter] is balance. With only 3 ingredients, daiquiris rely on striking that perfect balance between sweet and sour.


  1. Fill

    a cocktail shaker with ice and add rum, lime juice and simple syrup.

  2. Shake

    vigorously for at least 20 seconds.

  3. Strain

    into a chilled coupe glass and garnish with a lime wheel, if desired. Serve immediately.
